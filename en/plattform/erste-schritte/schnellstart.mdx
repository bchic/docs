<Steps>
<Step title="Create Your Account">
  Getting started is easy. First, create your bchic account.

  1. **Go to [bchic.de/anmelden](https://analytics.bchic.de/auth/sign-up) to create your free account.**
  2. Enter your website and your credentials.
  3. Click **"Create Account & Start Setup"** to begin the onboarding process.

</Step>

  Now, let's connect your website to bchic. Select your platform below to see the specific step-by-step instructions.

  <Tabs>
    <Tab id="wordpress" title="WordPress">
        **Instructions:**
        1. Log in to your WordPress dashboard.
        2. Navigate to `Plugins` > `Add New` and search for 'Insert Headers and Footers'.
        3. Install and activate the plugin.
        4. Go to the plugin's settings and paste the script into the 'Header' section.
        5. Save your changes.
        <Info>You can find your personal `data-website-id` in your onboarding flow.</Info>
    </Tab>
    <Tab id="webflow" title="Webflow">
        **Instructions:**

        This video shows the entire process in Webflow:
        <video className="my-4 rounded-md" autoPlay loop muted playsInline>
            <source src="/videos/webflow-guide.mp4" type="video/mp4" />
        </video>

        1. Open your project in the Webflow Designer.
        2. Click on 'Project Settings' in the top-left corner.
        3. Go to the 'Custom Code' tab.
        4. Paste the script into the 'Head Code' field.
        5. Save the changes and publish your site.
        <Info>You can find your personal `data-website-id` in your onboarding flow.</Info>
    </Tab>
    <Tab id="shopify" title="Shopify">
        **Instructions:**
        1. Log in to your Shopify admin panel.
        2. Go to `Online Store` > `Themes`.
        3. On your current theme, click `Actions` > `Edit code`.
        4. Open the `theme.liquid` file in the Layout directory.
        5. Paste the script right before the closing `</head>` tag.
        6. Click 'Save'.
        <Info>You can find your personal `data-website-id` in your onboarding flow.</Info>
    </Tab>
    <Tab id="squarespace" title="Squarespace">
        **Instructions:**
        1. In your Squarespace menu, go to `Settings` > `Advanced`.
        2. Select 'Code Injection'.
        3. Paste the script into the 'HEADER' text field.
        4. Click 'Save' in the top-left corner.
        <Info>You can find your personal `data-website-id` in your onboarding flow.</Info>
    </Tab>
    <Tab id="wix" title="Wix">
        **Instructions:**
        1. In your site's dashboard, go to `Settings`.
        2. Scroll down to the `Advanced` section and click 'Custom Code'.
        3. Click `+ Add Custom Code` in the 'Head' section.
        4. Paste the script and ensure it is set to load on all pages.
        5. Click 'Apply'.
        <Info>You can find your personal `data-website-id` in your onboarding flow.</Info>
    </Tab>
    <Tab id="framer" title="Framer">
        **Instructions:**
        1. Open your project in Framer.
        2. Go to `Settings` > `General`.
        3. Scroll to the `Custom Code` section.
        4. Paste the script into the 'End of `<head>` tag' field.
        5. Publish your site.
        <Info>You can find your personal `data-website-id` in your onboarding flow.</Info>
    </Tab>
    <Tab id="html" title="Other / HTML">
        **Instructions:**
        1. Open your website's HTML file in an editor.
        2. Find the closing `</head>` tag.
        3. Paste the script just before the `</head>` tag.
        4. Save the file and upload it to your server.
        <Info>You can find your personal `data-website-id` in your onboarding flow.</Info>
    </Tab>
  </Tabs>

<Step title="Verify the Installation">
  Great, the technical part is done. Now, let's check the connection.

  1. **Visit your own website** in a new browser tab to register the first visit.
  2. Return to the bchic onboarding flow and click the **"Verify Connection"** button.
  3. If successful, you'll be taken directly to your dashboard. Congratulations!

</Step>
