### Organization Name

Set the display name of your organization. This name is shown in the navigation and in team invitations.

Click **Edit** to update the name.

### Linked Websites

Under "Linked Website URLs" you can see all websites belonging to your organization. Each website gets its own dashboard with separate analytics data.

- **Add a website:** Click **+ Add Website** to link a new domain. You will receive a unique tracking code to embed on your site.
- **Manage a website:** Click on a website to access its specific settings such as tracking code, filters, and funnels.
